About Us

Homespot aims to put you in control of your home.
Providing remote lighting, heating and other Wi-Fi device control in your home for an affordable price.
We are a four-person team based in Worcester, UK.

Why Homespot?

Homespot replaces all the expensive and company specific hubs that you might ever need with one easy to use and simple hub.
The Homespot hub also come with software, available to use on your phone, tablet and desktop.


Features

While some smart home apps limit you to having on and off functionality to lights.
We aim to extend this by adding our own RGB colour picker, and brightness.
